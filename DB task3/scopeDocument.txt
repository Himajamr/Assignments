Restaurant Management System

Scope:
  This system provides service facility to restaurant and to the customer.
  Customer can visit the restaurant and order food. Customer table has their
  personal details. Every customer is assigned a unique id. when the customer 
  orders his food, he is assigned a particular waiter. 

  Orders table will have order id which is unique to each customer, customer 
  id, the food item the customer ordered i.e food id, order date and a boolean 
  variable is_paid to know if the customer paid the bill or not.

  Supplier table will have all the suppliers details and the supplies table will
  have the supplier details and the ingredients the supplier suppliers.
